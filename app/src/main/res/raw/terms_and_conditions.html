Terms and Conditions

This app is part of a user study conducted by University of Tartu for a Master's thesis on the topic of "Pervasive chatbots: Interpreting sensor data representations from the end users". By using this app, you agree to the following terms and conditions. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use this app.

Data Collection

The app logs and collects data related to your device usage. This includes:

  • Accessibility events
  • Application usage statistics
  • Package names of apps accessed
  • Timestamps of events
  • Types of events (e.g., clicks, swipes)
  • Other related metadata

This data is solely for research purposes and will not be used for any commercial intent.

Data Privacy

All data collected by the app will be anonymized and will not be linked to any personal identifiers. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we have implemented measures to ensure the confidentiality of your data.

Data Usage

The data collected will be used exclusively for academic research as part of a Master's thesis. It may be published in academic journals or presented at conferences, but it will always be in an aggregated and anonymized form.

Permissions

The app requires specific permissions to function correctly, including PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS and accessibility services. These permissions are essential for the data collection process.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation in this study is entirely voluntary. You can choose to stop using the app and withdraw from the study at any time without any consequences.

Risks and Benefits

There are no known risks associated with using this app. The benefits include contributing to academic research that may lead to advancements in the field of "Pervasive chatbots: Interpreting sensor data representations from the end users".
